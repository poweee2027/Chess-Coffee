Community

Join the Chess Coffee Community:

At Chess Coffee, we believe that the real joy of chess comes from connecting with others. Our vibrant community is filled with chess lovers from all over the globe, all coming together to share strategies, play games, and form friendships. Whether you're looking for opponents, mentors, or chess partners, you'll find it here. Sign up for free, and gain access to our online forums, where you can discuss chess openings, endgames, and everything in between.

Blog:

Our blog is your go-to resource for all things chess and coffee. From interviews with chess masters to deep dives into opening strategies, you’ll find content that inspires and educates. And if you're a coffee lover, don’t miss our brewing tips and tricks, product reviews, and behind-the-scenes looks at how we source and roast our coffee. Whether you're looking to improve your game or brew the perfect cup, our blog has something for you.

Special Events & Meetups:

In addition to our tournaments, we host regular in-person meetups at our Chess Coffee cafés. These gatherings are a chance for local players to meet up, play some games, and enjoy a great cup of coffee. We also organize special events like live-streamed games, grandmaster Q&A sessions, and chess-themed barista workshops where you can learn the art of making latte art inspired by your favorite chess pieces.
